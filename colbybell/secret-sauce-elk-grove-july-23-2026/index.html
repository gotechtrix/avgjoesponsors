The real secret sauce: it's personal

📣 Posts fill seats. People fill rooms.

Social posts create awareness, but the registrations almost always come from a direct, personal touch. A public post is easy to scroll past. A text with someone's name on it is not. The sponsors who pack the room are the ones who personally reach out to 20–40 people they actually know.

Treat this as a chance to add value to your network — you're handing them a genuinely useful business lunch, not asking for a favor. Aim for this mix:

📞Phone callsHighest conversion. Call your 10–15 best relationships personally. 60 seconds: "I'm sponsoring this, I'd love for you to be my guest."
💬TextsThe workhorse. Personal, hard to ignore, easy to reply. Send the templates below one-to-one — never as a mass blast.
✉️EmailsGreat for a broader list and people you reach professionally. Personalize the first line and send.
